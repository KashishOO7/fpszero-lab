## How to Use a Roadmap

1. **Read the entire roadmap first.** Get the map before you start walking.
2. **Pick your current level.** Be honest about it.
3. **Do the exercises, not just the reading.** Passive learning is fake learning.
4. **Track what you've done.** A text file, a notebook — anything. But track it.
5. **Revisit earlier phases.** After months of practice, Phase 0 will mean something completely different.
